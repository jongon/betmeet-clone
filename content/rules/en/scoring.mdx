## How are points awarded?

Every match you predict can give you points depending on how close you are to the actual score:

- **Exact score** (home and away): **5 points**.
- **Result** (winner or draw) without the exact score: **2 points**.
- **One team's goals** correct but missing the result: **1 point**.
- **No hit**: **0 points**.

In knockout stages, if you guess the penalty shootout winner, you earn **+1 additional point**.
