## How are points awarded?

Every match you predict can give you points depending on how close you are to the actual score:

- **Exact score** (home and away): **5 points**.
- If you don't hit the exact score, the following **stack up**:
  - **Correct result** (winner or draw): **+2 points**.
  - **Matched team goals**: **+1 point per team** (0, 1, or 2 points).
- **No hit**: **0 points**.

**Example**: actual result `BRA 2 - 1 ARG`, your prediction `BRA 3 - 2 ARG`. You got the winner (+2) and ARG's goals (+1). Total: **3 points**.

In knockout stages, if you guess the penalty shootout winner, you earn **+1 additional point**.
